About Me

My name is Jameson Bolish and I am an aspiring web designer. I've always been interested in technology and used it to create a couple different personal projects. I've been a bartender/server for around 15 years, making people laugh is my favorite thing to do, eventually I decided to do stand up comedy. After awhile I had an idea to create a robot head and do stand up comedy with audio and visual interaction. After four years of teaching myself soldering and microchips I finally made my debut show! I was curious on how websites were made and the different types of coding that are used to make web pages that I visit everyday.
